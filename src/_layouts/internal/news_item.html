Report on PPSA Review to be Tabled in Parliament

18 February 2015


Downloads

  •   PDF download link can go here  
  •   Another download link if required  

Macks Advisory suggests business owners, managers and their advisers keep a sharp lookout for the report on a review of the Personal Property Securities Act 2009 (PPSA) that should be tabled in the Federal Parliament no later than 18 March 2015. Thereafter the report, required by section 343 of the Act, can be seen on the Parliament of Australia website.
