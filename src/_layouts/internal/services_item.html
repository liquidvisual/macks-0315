Mortgagee in Possession

Sizeable non-contiguous land holding of approx 8,000 acres of farm land around Burra, SA.

Background

- Sizeable non-contiguous land holding of approx. 8,000 acres of farm land around Burra, SA - Land held by well-known local family for 100+ years - Relationship with financier soured with owners initiating proceedings and fighting possession orders

Action

- Assisted financier to take possession and secure assets - Assessed condition of land and utilised appropriate experts - Appointed property manager for daily presence and efficiency - Managed 600 head of cattle on properties until ownership dispute settled - Facilitated lease of property to experienced operator to provide income, maintain soil conditioning and improve appearance for sale - Tendered for and appointed local sales agent - Realised surplus assets and supplies

Result

- Strong auction results for properties - Realised proceeds from healthy herd of cattle with few maintenance issues
